Catalog Priorities

The catalog is being strengthened through context, specificity, and care.

Culturally responsive cataloging

Records are being revised to move beyond generic labels and to foreground culture, function, context, and provenance wherever the available evidence allows.

Research-centered access

The collection supports teaching, student research, curatorial study, and future collections-management work through a clearer and more intentional metadata model.

Single-source metadata

The site reads from one MDL-aligned master catalog source, keeping catalog development and public presentation in closer alignment.
