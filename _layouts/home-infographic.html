Current Priorities

The catalog is being strengthened through context, specificity, and care.

Culturally responsive cataloging

Records are being revised to move beyond generic labels and to foreground culture, function, context, and provenance wherever the available evidence allows.

Research-centered access

The collection supports teaching, student research, and curatorial study through clearer and more intentional record writing.

Shared record system

The site and the editing workspace now draw from the same core record structure, helping public presentation and catalog work stay aligned.
